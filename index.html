ATEEZ (에이티즈),
known pre-debut as KQ Fellaz, is an 8-member boy group under KQ Entertainment. The group consists of: Seonghwa, Hongjoong, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung and Jongho. They released the pre-debut song ‘From’ on July 9, 2018 with another trainee named Junyoung. The group debuted on October 24, 2018 with the mini-album “Treasure Ep.1: All to Zero” and the title songs ‘Pirate King’ and ‘Treasure’. On July 8, 2019, ATEEZ signed with USA record label RCA Records. They are also signed to the Japanese record label Nippon Columbia since 2019.
